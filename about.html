About Me

Hi, I'm John Hampton. I'm a front-end web developer with a strong focus on design and web user interfaces that work for everyone and make sense. I'm skilled in HTML5/CSS, SASS, Javascript, jQuery, AngularJS, EmberJS, and NodeJS. I'm also a graduate of The Iron Yard's Front End Engineering Course. In my spare time I enjoy electronic music, mixed martial arts, and fine German riesling.
